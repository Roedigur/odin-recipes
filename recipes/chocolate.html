Chocolate

I don't think there needs to be a long description over here. It's chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Cacao butter
  • Dry milk powder
  • Cacao powder
  • Salt
  • Powdred sugar

Instructions

  1. Melt the cacao butter in a pan.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients.
  3. Mold the milk chocolate.

Tip: You can make your own hot chocolate with the chocolate you just made! Click here for the hot chocolate recipe: Hot Chocolate

You're done! It wasn't that hard, right? Now it is time to enjoy your self made chocolate!
