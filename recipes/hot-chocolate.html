Hot Chocolate

Homemade hot chocolate is very easy to make and can be done very fast. By adding cocoa powder it gives the hot chocolate a real chocolate taste.

Ingredients

  • Cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract
Optional:
  • whipped cream

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together the sugar and cocoa powder. Add milk, chocolate chunks, and vanilla.
  2. Place over medium heat until steaming, whisking constantly to avoid burning the chocolate.
  3. Cool to desired serving temperature and serve with your favorite toppings.
Optional: Put some whipped cream on your hot chocolate.

Tip: You can use your self made chocolate for the chocolate milk. Don't know how to make chocolate? Check it out here: Chocolate
